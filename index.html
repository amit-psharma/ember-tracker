Why 25 minutes?

The Pomodoro Technique uses 25-minute intervals of highly focused, uninterrupted work followed by a 5-minute break. This duration is scientifically proven to be long enough to achieve deep work, but short enough to prevent cognitive fatigue.

Habit Science

1% Better Every Day

Based on the principles of Atomic Habits

1

Make it Obvious

Design your environment. The cues that trigger your habits should be front and center.

2

Make it Attractive

Pair an action you want to do with an action you need to do (Temptation Bundling).

3

Make it Easy

Reduce friction. The 2-Minute Rule states any new habit should take less than 2 minutes to start.

4

Make it Satisfying

What is immediately rewarded is repeated. Never miss twice. Form the 21-day chain.
